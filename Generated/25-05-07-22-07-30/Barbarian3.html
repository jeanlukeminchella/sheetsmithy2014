RAGE - O O O

• You take half non-magical damage.

• You may enter Rage as a Bonus Action.

• You have advantage on Athletics checks and Strength saving throws.

• You may add 2 to weapon damage.

• Rages last one minute, but end early if you fall unconcious. You must also attack or take damage each round.

• When you enter rage, roll a d8 to unleash a magical effect. (Wild Magic - see TCoE for effects)

GRAPPLING

• You may only perform Javelin attacks while grappling.

• You move at half speed, and the grappled creature moves with you.

• The grappled creature's speed becomes 0

• You may release the target at any time.
